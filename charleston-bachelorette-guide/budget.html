Charleston Bachelorette Budget Planning

Transparent cost breakdowns for every celebration style

  1. Home
  2. Charleston Bachelorette Guide
  3. Budget Planning

Charleston bachelorette parties accommodate budgets from $500 to $3,000+ per person depending on accommodation choices, dining preferences, and activity selections. This guide provides realistic cost breakdowns helping you plan celebrations matching your financial comfort while maximizing fun and memorable experiences.

Budget Planning Essentials:
  • Weekend costs vary dramatically by accommodation choice (beach house vs. hotel)
  • Dining decisions are the biggest variable expense factor
  • Free and low-cost activities can create amazing experiences
  • Clear communication about budget expectations prevents awkwardness

Budget Tier Breakdown (Per Person, 3-Day Weekend)

Budget-Friendly

$500-750

Total per person

Accommodation: $150-250

Folly Beach rental split among 10-12 guests, or budget hotel rooms split between roommates

Food & Dining: $150-200

Cook 5-6 meals at rental, eat at budget restaurants like Taco Boy, Chico Feo, casual spots

Activities: $50-100

Free beach time, DIY games, one paid activity like food tour or brewery crawl

Nightlife: $75-125

Drink groceries at rental, hit budget bars in NoMo or Folly Beach, skip expensive clubs

Transportation: $30-75

Minimal Ubers, mostly stay at beach, maybe one downtown trip

Best For: Cost-conscious groups, younger crowds, casual celebrations

Mid-Range

$1,000-1,500

Total per person

Accommodation: $300-500

Isle of Palms beach house mid-tier rental or downtown hotel near Upper King Street

Food & Dining: $300-450

Mix of cooking at rental and restaurants. One upscale dinner, casual lunches, nice brunches

Activities: $150-250

Food/wine tours, water sports rentals, mobile spa services, professional photos

Nightlife: $150-250

Upper King bar crawl, rooftop bars, some bottle service or VIP experiences

Transportation: $100-150

Multiple Uber trips between beach and downtown, rideshares for nightlife

Best For: Most bachelorette groups, balanced experience, popular choice

Luxury

$2,000-3,000+

Total per person

Accommodation: $600-1,000

Luxury oceanfront Isle of Palms estate, or The Dewberry/Hotel Bennett in individual rooms

Food & Dining: $500-800

Private chef dinners, top Charleston restaurants (Husk, FIG), upscale brunches, full catering

Activities: $400-700

Private yacht charter, full spa days, professional entertainment, photography, cooking classes

Nightlife: $300-500

VIP bottle service at clubs, premium bars, extensive bar crawls, top-shelf everything

Transportation: $200-300

Private car service, party bus rental, unlimited rideshares without budget concerns

Best For: Ultimate experiences, larger budgets, milestone celebrations

Communicating Budget with Your Group

Clear budget conversations prevent awkwardness and ensure everyone can participate comfortably. Send detailed breakdown early (8 weeks before), itemize every cost, offer payment plans if needed, and make expensive activities optional. Create shared spreadsheet tracking who paid what, and be transparent about total expected costs upfront.
