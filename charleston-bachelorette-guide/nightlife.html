Charleston nightlife centers around Upper King Street, creating a walkable bar district perfect for bachelorette party bar crawls. From sophisticated rooftop lounges with harbor views to high-energy dance clubs, Charleston offers diverse evening experiences matching every celebration style.

This comprehensive nightlife guide covers Charleston's best bars, clubs, and entertainment venues for bachelorette parties, including recommended bar crawl routes, dress codes, cover charges, and insider tips for maximizing your night out in the Holy City.

Charleston Nightlife Quick Facts:
  • Prime Nightlife Area: Upper King Street (north of Calhoun to Spring Street)
  • Peak Nights: Thursday-Saturday (Fridays busiest)
  • Bar Hours: Most bars open until 2 AM (South Carolina law)
  • Dress Code: Smart casual to upscale (no athletic wear most places)
  • Uber/Lyft: Abundant but surge pricing on weekend nights
  • Walking Distance: Most bars within 0.5 mile radius on Upper King
