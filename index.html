Popular Charleston Experiences

Walking Tours

Explore Charleston’s streets, pastel houses, and hidden alleys with a fun guide.

Charleston is best discovered on foot, and walking tours are a favorite for bachelorette groups who want to keep it cute while learning a little something.

  • The Flirty Stroll Tour – pastel houses, cobblestones, flower boxes, and fun local tea.
  • Food & Cocktail Tour – she-crab soup, benne wafers, bourbon, and more.
  • Custom Bride Tour – tailored routes around sweets, shopping, or bar-hopping.

Pro tip: Skip the heels — comfy sandals make the experience way better.

Carriage Rides

Experience Charleston in a horse-drawn carriage with photo ops galore.

Charleston’s iconic carriage tours deliver Southern charm in the sweetest way.

  • Sit back and relax – no walking required!
  • Photo ops – Rainbow Row, The Battery, and the bride’s “bridal chariot.”
  • Private rides – so your group can sip iced coffee or mimosas together.
Harbor Cruises

Enjoy sunsets, sea breezes, and unforgettable moments on the water.

Every bachelorette weekend needs a wow moment — and a harbor cruise delivers.

  • Private Charter – your music, your drinks, your vibe.
  • Sunset Sail – golden hour glow and city skyline views.
  • Party Pontoon – casual fun with coolers and Bluetooth speakers.

Tip: Book early for peak season weekends.

Charleston Bachelorette Tour Ideas

Discover Charleston on Foot

Charleston is best discovered on foot, and walking tours are a favorite for bachelorette groups who want to keep it cute while learning a little something (or pretending to while sipping cocktails).

Top picks for bride squads:
  • The Flirty Stroll Tour – Pastel houses, cobblestone alleys, flower-filled window boxes, and a local guide who knows how to dish Charleston tea with flair. This isn’t a history class—it’s a curated stroll through the city’s most photogenic spots with plenty of time for selfies and sips.
  • The Charleston Food & Cocktail Tour – Taste your way through local cuisine—she-crab soup, benne wafers, and maybe even a bourbon or two. The bride eats for free if you’re lucky with the right host.
  • Custom Bride Walking Tour – Private tours themed around your bride’s style—whether that’s sweets, shopping, scandalous stories, or bar-hopping fun.

Pro tip: Skip the heels—opt for cute, comfy sandals that can handle Charleston’s historic streets. Walking tours are also a sneaky fun way to keep the party moving!

Add Royal Vibes with a Carriage Ride

Want something straight out of a fairytale? Charleston’s iconic horse-drawn carriage tours deliver romantic Southern charm in the slowest, sweetest way possible. Perfect for a daytime activity when everyone’s recharging but still wants to explore.

Why it’s perfect for bachelorettes:
  • Sit back and relax – no walking required!
  • Perfect photo ops – in front of Rainbow Row, The Battery, and the bride beaming on her “bridal chariot.”
  • Private carriage options – so your group can ride together, sipping iced coffee or to-go mimosas.

Whether you’re soaking in antebellum mansions or waving to tourists from your elevated seat, a carriage ride makes your crew feel like Charleston queens.

Make Waves with a Harbor Cruise

Every bachelorette weekend needs one “wow” moment—and a harbor cruise is it. Charleston Harbor offers sparkling views, stunning sunsets, and sea breezes that give you that bridal glow.

Three cruise options to love:
  • Private Luxury Charter – Go all out with a private boat. You choose the music, bring your favorite bubbly, and maybe even surprise the bride with a performer onboard.
  • Sunset Sail – Romantic and relaxed with golden hour lighting, gentle vibes, and views of the city skyline, Ravenel Bridge, and waterfront mansions.
  • Party Pontoons – Budget-friendly boats with coolers and Bluetooth speakers. Just BYO snacks, drinks, and wild energy.

Don’t forget: Book early—spring and summer weekends fill up fast with bachelorette traffic.

Why These Tours Work for Bachelorette Groups

Charleston’s walking tours, carriage rides, and harbor cruises give you variety, style, and a built-in way to explore the city without doing all the planning yourself. They also:

  • Create bonding moments – whether it’s giggling in a carriage, dancing on a boat deck, or sharing hushpuppies on a walking food tour.
  • Keep it balanced – active enough to be fun, but relaxed enough that nobody feels rushed.
  • Fit every budget – with options for private or group tours, it’s easy to find something that works for your squad.
