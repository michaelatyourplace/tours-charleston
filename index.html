Say Charleston About Charleston Tours

Charleston Walking Tours, Harbor Cruises & Carriage Rides Perfect for Bachelorettes

Planning a Charleston bachelorette celebration? Say hello to the perfect mix of Southern charm, ocean breeze, and flirty fun. Whether your bride tribe loves strolling through pastel neighborhoods, cruising the harbor with champagne in hand, or riding high in a horse-drawn carriage like Southern royalty, Charleston has something unforgettable in store. Our primary location is Charleston, South Carolina. From cobblestone struts to sunset sails, here’s your go-to guide for walking tours, harbor cruises, and carriage rides made for brides and besties.

Strut Through History with a Flirty Walking Tour

Charleston is best discovered on foot, and walking tours are a favorite for bachelorette groups who want to keep it cute while learning a little something (or pretending to while sipping cocktails).

Top picks for bride squads:
  • The Flirty Stroll Tour - Think pastel houses, cobblestone alleys, flower-filled window boxes, and a local guide who knows how to dish Charleston tea with flair. This isn’t a history class—it’s a curated stroll through the city’s most photogenic spots with plenty of time for selfies and sips.
  • The Charleston Food & Cocktail Tour - Bond over bites as you taste your way through local cuisine—she-crab soup, benne wafers, and maybe even a bourbon or two. The bride eats for free if you’re lucky with the right host.
  • Custom Bride Walking Tour - Want a totally tailored experience? Some guides offer private tours themed around your bride’s style—whether that’s sweets, shopping, scandalous stories, or bar-hopping fun.

Pro tip: Charleston’s beauty is in the details, so skip the heels and opt for cute, comfy sandals that can handle historic streets. Tours are also great for a bachelorette partay.

2. Add Royal Vibes with a Carriage Ride

Want something straight out of a fairytale? Charleston’s iconic horse-drawn carriage tours deliver romantic Southern charm in the slowest, sweetest way possible. Perfect for a daytime activity when everyone’s recharging but still wants to explore.

Why it’s perfect for bachelorettes:
  • Sit back and relax - no walking required!
  • Perfect photo ops - in front of Rainbow Row, The Battery, and the bride beaming on her ‘bridal chariot.’
  • Private carriage options - available, so your group can ride together, sipping iced coffee or to-go mimosas.

Whether you're soaking in antebellum mansions or waving to tourists from your elevated seat, a carriage tour is elegant, slow-paced, and makes your crew feel like Charleston queens.

Make Waves with a Harbor Cruise

Every bachelorette weekend needs one “wow” moment—and a harbor cruise is it. Charleston Harbor offers sparkling views, stunning sunsets, and sea breeze that gives you that bridal glow.

Three cruise options to love:
  • Private Luxury Charter Go all out with a private boat for your group. You choose the music, bring your favorite bubbly, and maybe even surprise the bride with a performer onboard. Hello, unforgettable moment.
  • Sunset Sail Want something romantic and relaxed? Sunset cruises give you golden hour lighting, gentle vibes, and a slow drift past the city skyline. Bonus: You’ll get amazing pics of the Ravenel Bridge and historic homes along the waterfront.
  • Party Pontoons These budget-friendly, fun-first boats are perfect for casual brides. Some come equipped with coolers and Bluetooth speakers—just BYO snacks, drinks, and wild energy.

Don’t forget:Book in advance, especially during spring and summer weekends when bachelorette traffic is at its peak.
